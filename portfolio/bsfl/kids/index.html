    Features

    • Each Leader Guide contains user-friendly session plans that give specific ways kids can apply the concepts they've learned to their lives and a reminder of how each Bible story points to Christ
    • Engaging Kids Activity Pages
    • One Conversation™: A helpful overview on the back of each Kids Activity Page that makes it easy for parents to lead one spiritual conversation each week
    • Dynamic weekly videos that help kids connect and apply biblical concepts to life
    • Scripture memory songs that make each verse easy and fun to learn
    • Coloring pages and other time savers for leaders
    • Family App to engage kids and parents in Bible learning together
    • Visual Pack Posters that bring the lessons to life
    • Bonus Teaching Hour: Large Group Experience is now included on your Enhanced CD. Use this for an extra hour of teaching, kids worship, or creating a large group experience before moving to closely graded classes
    • Quick Start Kit - Includes one each of the Kids Activity Pages, Leader Guide, Leader Pack, Enhanced CD, and DVD filled with Life Action clips. Order one for each class.
    • New! Large Group Worship DVD-ROM: Wow kids with worship just for them! Includes step-by-step teaching plans, relevant video clips, music videos with lyrics, leader helps, and files for multimedia use. View a sample.

    Bible Studies for Life: Kids

    Our studies for preschool and grade school ages will lead kids to know and connect with the God of the Bible. They'll grow to understand that He is real and relevant to their everyday lives. Imagine kids learning to not only seek the truth through Scripture but also being challenged each week to apply those truths to their lives. Imagine kids seeing God as the Great I Am, not some fictional character they can take or leave.

    Study Plan:

    Bible Studies for Life follows an ongoing study plan addressing key life issues and needs. You can view the first 3 years of the plan here.

    • Year Two
    • Year Three

    Translation and Format Options:

    • Multiple translations available
    • Multiple delivery options: Print, digital, and app

    Strengthen Families:

    • Kids study the same biblical concept, or "Life Point", each week as the rest of their family – from babies to adults.
    • All-church alignment, twice per year – In addition to studying the same concepts each week, twice a year kids studies fully align with students and adult resources to make church-wide campaigns even easier to do.
    • Every Kids Activity Page is filled with a helpful overview that connects the content from the adult, student, and kids session. Bible Studies for Life: Kids makes it easy for parents to keep the entire family engaged in "One Conversation" surrounding Scripture.
