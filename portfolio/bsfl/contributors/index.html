    Meet Our Contributors

    To ensure Bible Studies for Life is consistently meeting the needs of your church, we involve pastors, age-level specialists, and gifted writers in the process of creating each study. Our Advisory Council includes Christian leaders from across the country who consulted with our team on the development of the scope, sequence, and content of Bible Studies for Life.

    Editors

    Ronnie Floyd
    GENERAL EDITOR

    Pastor for more than 36 years. In 26 years of ministry in Northwest Arkansas, Cross Church has baptized more 17,000 people. Author of 20 books, chairman of the SBC Great Commission Resurgence Task Force, he and his wife Jeana have two sons and six grandchildren.

    @ronniefloyd
    RonnieFloyd.com

    Mark Howell
    SMALL GROUP ADVISOR

    Pastor of Communities at Canyon Ridge Christian Church in Las Vegas. Founder, consultant, coach of SmallGroupResources.net. Served in staff positions across America for more than 25 years. He and his wife Debbie have three children and three grandchildren.

    @markchowell
    MarkHowellLive.com

    David Francis
    MANAGING EDITOR

    Author of many influential books regarding Sunday School and discipleship. Served on church staff for more than 18 years in associate pastor and minister of education positions. He and his wife Vickie have three sons and four grandchildren.

    @1davidfrancis
    LifeWay.com/DavidFrancis

    Writers

    Chip Henderson

    Senior Pastor of Pinelake Church in Brandon, Miss., which has grown from 700 to more than 9000 people on four campuses. Co-creator of the L3 Journal. Ph.D. in New Testament Studies. He and his wife Christy have three children.

    Ron Edmondson

    Senior Pastor for Immanuel Baptist Church in Lexington, Ky. Passionate about helping people fully grasp the depth of God's love and the power of His grace through his roles as pastor, blogger, and church planter. Loves to read, write, dream and run ...sometimes. He and his wife Cheryl have two sons.

    K. Marshall Williams, Sr.

    Pastor for 28 years of the historic Nazarene Baptist Church in Philadelphia. Chaplain work with Philadelphia Phillies, Eagles, and 76ers. President of the Baptist Convention of Pennsylvania/South Jersey (2006-2007) and Vice President of the National African American Fellowship (SBC). He and wife Constance have three children.

    Philip Nation

    Director of Adult Ministry Publishing with LifeWay Christian Resources and frequent speaker in churches and conferences. Serves as a teaching pastor for The Fellowship in Nashville. Has written and edited numerous books and articles. He and his wife Angie have two sons.

    Ben Mandrell

    Senior pastor of Englewood Baptist Church in Jackson, Tenn., a growing fellowship of believers focused on the Great Commandment and the Great Commission. He and his wife Lynley have four children and enjoy teaching the principles for healthy marriage.

    Robert Jeffress

    Senior Pastor of First Baptist Church of Dallas. He has written 19 books and can be heard nationwide on more than 725 stations with his daily radio program, Pathway to Victory. His bold, biblical, and practical approach to ministry has made him one of the country's most respected evangelical leaders. He and his wife Amy have two daughters.

    Gregg Matte

    Senior pastor of Houston's First Baptist Church. Under his leadership, this cutting edge ministry has experienced tremendous growth. Gregg also founded Breakaway Ministries — the largest college Bible study in the nation. He is an author of many books and Bible studies. He and his wife Kelly have a son and a daughter.

    Nick Floyd

    Campus Pastor at Cross Church in Fayetteville, Arkansas. He preaches weekly in four different services to this growing and exciting congregation. Nick is also an avid football fan. He and his wife, Meredith, have three children.

    Pete Wilson

    Founding and Senior Pastor of Cross Point Church in Nashville, TN. Cross Point is one of America's fastest growing churches with an attendance of more than 5,000 people through its four campuses. Pete is also an author and national conference speaker. He and his wife Brandi have three sons.
