Bible Study Content for Preschoolers

Day 1: One Amazing Creator

Scripture: Genesis 1:1–2:3

Bible Story: God Made All Things

Verse: Everything God made was very good. Genesis 1:31

Life Application: God loves us and made all things.

Day 2: One Miraculous Provider

Scripture: 2 Kings 4:1-7

Bible Story: God Helps Us

Verse: God is good to us. Psalm 73:1

Life Application: God loves us and helps us.

Day 3: One Powerful Healer

Scripture: Matthew 9:1-8,27-31

Bible Story: God Cares for Us

Verse: Jesus loves you. John 15:12

Life Application: God loves and cares for us.

Day 4: One Living Savior

Scripture: Luke 24:36-49

Bible Story: God Gave Us Jesus

Verse: God loves us and sent His Son. 1 John 4:10

Life Application: God loves us and gave us Jesus.

Day 5: One Lord of All

Scripture: Acts 10:24-48

Bible Story: God Loves All People

Verse: Love one another. 1 John 4:7

Life Application: God loves us and wants us to love all people.

Bible Study Content for Children

Day 1: One Amazing Creator

Scripture: Genesis 1:1–2:3

Bible Story: God Created the World

Verse: Psalm 104:24

Life Application: Because God is Creator, I can praise Him and care for His world.

Day 2: One Miraculous Provider

Scripture: 2 Kings 4:1-7,38-44

Bible Story: God Provided for Elisha and His People

Verse: 1 Timothy 6:17

Life Application: Because God is Provider, I can depend on Him and share with others.

Day 3: One Powerful Healer

Scripture: Matthew 9:1-8,18-33

Bible Story: Jesus Healed

Verse: Luke 6:19

Life Application: Because God is Healer, I can thank Him and show care for others.

Day 4: One Living Savior

Scripture: Luke 24:1-12,36-49

Bible Story: Jesus Is Alive!

Verse: 1 John 4:14

Life Application: Because God sent Jesus to be the Savior of the world, I can trust Jesus as my Savior and Lord and tell others about Him.

Day 5: One Lord of All

Scripture: Acts 10:24-43

Bible Story: Peter Visits Cornelius’ Home

Verse: Acts 10:36

Life Application: I can follow God and show respect to all people.
