CHILDREN'S DAY–AT–A–GLANCE

Kids will focus on the same Scripture verse each day! 2 Timothy 1:7

day 1

DARE TO CHANGE

Missions:

The Pillays dared to change when God led them to move from South Africa to share the gospel in America.

The Flook family is being used by God in Bolivia because they dared to change.

Bible Story: Paul Obeyed (Acts 9:1-18)

Life Application: I can trust God and obey Him without fear.

Crafts:

  • Fireworks Pen
  • Colossal Coaster Card
  • Fireworks T-shirt

“Change My Heart”

day 2

DARE TO SPEAK UP

Missions:

The Pillays trusted God to help them speak boldly about Jesus even in a scary place.

The Flooks use prayerwalking as a big part of their ministry to speak boldly about Jesus in Bolivia.

Bible Story: Paul Spoke Boldly (Acts 9:20-30)

Life Application: I can trust God to help me speak boldly about Him.

Crafts:

  • Basket Toss
  • Souvenir Basket
  • Ferris Wheel Puzzle

“Speak Up (Tell the World)”

day 3

DARE TO BELIEVE

Missions:

Shaun Pillay shared the gospel with David Holland and because David dared to believe, his life has changed forever.

The Flook family shares their story with the people they meet through music and by learning to speak Spanish.

Bible Story: Paul Proclaimed Salvation (Acts 16:16-34)

Life Application: I can trust God’s plan of salvation and share His love with others.

Crafts:

  • Roller Coaster Maze
  • ABC Tilt-a-Whirl Top
  • Prison Earthquake Illusion

“The Ride of Your Life”

day 4

DARE TO STAND STRONG

Missions:

Shaun Pillay and David Holland share the love of Jesus with everyone they meet as they lean on each other and dare to stand strong.

Stephen and Samuel Flook share Jesus’ love at Bible studies with their dad. Sarah, Hannah, and Mary Margaret share Jesus’ love in their gymnastics class.

Bible Story: Paul Responded Wisely (Acts 21:27-23:11)

Life Application: I can trust God to help me have self-control and honor Him with my actions.

Crafts:

  • Bible Verse Sticky-note Holder
  • Roller Coaster Coaster
  • Ticket Frame

“Stand Strong”

day 5

DARE TO TRUST

Missions:

The Pillays trusted God and He gave them the power to face their fears and start a church in Norwich, Connecticut.

The Flooks are still praying and trusting God for new churches to start in Bolivia.

Bible Story: Paul Trusted God (Acts 27)

Life Application: I can trust God to give me the power to face my fears no matter what comes next.

Crafts:

  • Fear Face Bookmark
  • Faux Spin Art Autograph Book
  • Storm-tossed Ship Paperweight

“I Will Trust in You”
