VBX Preteen Bible Study Leader Pack Correction

The directions for the "Track It Cars" (pack item 8) state to cut the cars apart and fold in the tabs. There are no tabs on the cars. Cut the cars out and tape them to the "Track It Poster" (pack item 9) as instructed in the leader guide. The leader guide states that you may not use all 12 cars, however there are a total of 18 cars.
