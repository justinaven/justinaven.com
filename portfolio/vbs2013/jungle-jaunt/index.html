Jump into a Vacation Bible School experience as big as the Amazon rainforest!

Each day of Club VBS®: Jungle Jaunt™ kids will discover the One True God, and you'll have the opportunity to challenge kids to respond to Jesus as the Living Savior and Lord of All. If you're looking for an anytime Vacation Bible School that's flexible and easy to use, Club VBS is for you. It works great for any size VBS from small to large!

VBS Motto

Praise Him! Trust Him! Follow Him! The One True God!

VBS Scriptures

Psalm 154:1-2

It's Jam-Packed with FUN!

Club VBS 2013 Starter Kit

Take the first step in planning your Jungle Jaunt expedition. The Starter Kit has everything you need to begin in one box, simply add the number of additional Learner Cards and Leader Guides you need and you'll be on your way!

Club VBS is ideal for:

  • Traditional Week-long Summer Vacation Bible School
  • Spring Break Event
  • Fall Break Event
  • Day Camps
  • Mission Trips
  • Mid-week Programs
  • Backyard Bible Clubs
